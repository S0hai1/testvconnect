Strict Qualifications and Compliance

At Value Connect Solutions, we have established strict qualifications to ensure that our agents are well-prepared to meet the demands of their roles. All successful candidates must have at least one year of college-level education, providing them with foundational knowledge and critical thinking skills.

Additionally, candidates must have prior contact center experience relevant to the position they are applying for. This experience ensures they bring a practical understanding of the industry and job, and with our training, they can contribute effectively from day one.

To further guarantee the integrity and reliability of our staff, we require all successful candidates to obtain clearance from local authorities and complete medical screening tests to confirm they are physically and mentally fit for the role.

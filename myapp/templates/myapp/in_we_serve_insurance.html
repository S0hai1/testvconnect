Why Do Insurance Companies Outsource?

There are many reasons why insurance companies and agencies choose to outsource various functions of their company. Here are just a few:

Reducing Staff Cost

Saving money is one of the primary reasons insurance companies outsource their claims and customer care services to companies outside the USA. The cost of hiring quality, trained, and college-educated staff in the India is much more affordable than hiring and training local talent.

Alleviating Work Pressure

Are your employees wearing too many hats? If your staff is assigned the daily tasks of claims processing or customer care, they likely don't have the time to focus on the strategic part of their job. Hiring an external staff to take over tedious tasks such as claims processing will free up time and headspace for your team to do what they do best.

Ability to Quickly Scale Up

Events like natural disasters and acts of terrorism can cause a drastic spike in claims. Suppose your exclusive support team is internal—staff. In that case, your members can quickly become overwhelmed, and claims can drag on for months or even years, creating discontent among policyholders and giving your company a bad name. Partnering with a strategic BPO provider provides access to a large team of trained individuals to help you and your customers get back on track.

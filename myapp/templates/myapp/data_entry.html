Data Entry: A Key Area for Cost Savings

Data entry is essential but often viewed as a mundane task that consumes valuable time for sales and support agents. Studies show that up to 10% of a call center agent's time is spent on after-call data entry tasks, costing the industry over $20 billion annually. A 500-seat call center handling 5 million calls annually could spend over $2 million on wrap-up time alone. Reducing this time by just 25% can save over $500,000 annually. Additional savings can come from reduced agent turnover, lower training costs, shorter customer wait times, and improved customer satisfaction.

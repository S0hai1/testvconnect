What Is Lead Generation?

Lead generation is the process of identifying individuals or businesses interested in your company's products or services. Leads can be generated through various channels, like:

  • search engines
  • social media
  • your company website
  • inbound calls
  • Cold calling campaigns

Effective lead generation bridges the gap between your marketing efforts and sales goals, ensuring that your team connects with prospects ready to convert.

The Benefits of Lead Generation Services
